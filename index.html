    • Internal Audit risk and compliance services

    Internal Audit, Risk & Compliance Services (IARCS) integrate corporate governance from the areas of internal audit, enterprise risk managemnet, compliance and sustainability.

    Using our proprietary internal audit methodology (IAM), we analyze an organization's business strategies and related business processes to help managemnt understand strategic risks and identify cost reduction and profit improvement opportunities.

    Our risk-based strategy can help organizations transform their internal audit and risk management functions from a traditional, transactions based approach into one that can deliver strategic business assurance and value.

    Internal Audit services (IAS) provide an integrated approach to internal control and business risk management. An analysis of the company's strategic objectives and the processes used to achieve them is performed by using appropriate tools and practices in an effort to support management in recognizing strategic risks and managing them effectively, in applying effective corporate governance methods and practices, and in organizing an effective internal control system.

    Internal Audit Startegic Sourcing

    Co-sourcing/outsourcing: Selah Peritus' experienced professionals can assist organizations with such processes as risk assessment, audit plan development, internal control procedures documentation, audit programs development, internal audit planning and execution, identification of deficiencies and weaknesses and recommendations for improvements.

    The objective is to support the management in pursuing continuous improvement of internal control and operations. The above services can be fully or partly outsourced or co-sourced to selah peritus. Co-sourcing additionally aims at transferring knowledge to the company's internal audit department.

    Continuous Auditing & Continuous Monitoring

    Leveraging Selah Peritus' methods, tools and professionals to implement or enhance the automated collection of audit evidence and feedback mechanism for management to ensure that the systems and controls have been operating as designed and transactions are porcessed appropriately.

    The goal of CA/CM is to provide greater transparency into operations and more timely reporting of concerns. There are a number of drivers for CA/CM. They include the pressure to improve governance, the need for better visisbility into global operations, the occurence of fraud and misconduct, and the expanding regulatory and risk environment.

    In supporting an organization's efforts to address these drivers, some of the benefits CA/CM offers can include:

  • Delivering better information management.
  • Delivering regular insight into the status of controls and transactions.
  • Timely identifications of errors or fraud.
  • Reporting value to the Board and executive management teams.
